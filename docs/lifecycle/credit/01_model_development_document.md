# MODEL DEVELOPMENT DOCUMENT

## Purpose & Intended Use

The purpose of this model development document is to provide a comprehensive overview of the credit default (PD) scorecard model, its development process, and its intended use. The model is designed to estimate the probability of default (PD) for consumer loan applications, supporting credit decisioning under ECOA/Reg B and FCRA fair-lending constraints.

## Data & Features

The model was developed using a dataset containing 1200 samples, with 840 samples used for training and 360 samples used for testing. The dataset includes the following features:

*   `dti`: Debt-to-income ratio
*   `utilization`: Credit utilization ratio
*   `delinq_2yr`: Number of delinquencies in the past 2 years
*   `inquiries_6m`: Number of credit inquiries in the past 6 months
*   `annual_income`: Annual income of the applicant
*   `loan_amount`: Amount of the loan being applied for
*   `emp_length_yrs`: Length of employment in years
*   `num_open_accounts`: Number of open accounts
*   `is_homeowner`: Indicator for whether the applicant is a homeowner
